FAQ

How Much Does It Cost?

Nothing, it’s totally free. Registration and attendance are 100% free, with meals, drinks, swag, & workshops included.

Who Can Participate?

Right now only Rutgers students can attend, but in the future we plan to open it up to all students.

What If I Never Coded Before?

All skill levels are welcome. Though we primarily market to beginners, we welcome experts (& everyone in between).

What If I Know How To Code?

All skill levels are welcome. Though we primarily market to beginners, we welcome experts (& everyone in between).

What Can I Make?

Anything! A website, app, game, hardware, you name it. Judges will rate projects on creativity , technical skill demonstrated, polish, & utility.

What Should I Bring?

Student ID, computer, chargers, anything for your hack (hardware?)

Is This A Team Event?

Yep! You’ll have an opportunity to meet fellow hackers and make teams of up to 4—find some new friends, bring yours, or work alone.

Who Runs And/Or Supervises This Event?

Rutgers Camden ACM Organization runs and supervises this event.
